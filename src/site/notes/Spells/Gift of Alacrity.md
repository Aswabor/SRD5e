# Gift of Alacrity
*A 1st Level Divination Spell.*

**Casting Time:** 1 Minute

**Range:** Point, Touch

**Components:** V S 

**Duration:** Timed, 8 Hours

You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target can add {@dice 1d8} to its initiative rolls.
