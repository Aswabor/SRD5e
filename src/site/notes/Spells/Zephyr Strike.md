# Zephyr Strike
*A 1st Level Transmutation Spell.*

**Casting Time:** 1 Bonus

**Range:** Point, Self

**Components:** V 

**Duration:** Timed, 1 Minute

You move like the wind. Until the spell ends, your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks.
